Block Shift Capital Investment Process

01

Sourcing and Screening

Sourcing : Actively seek out promising startups and projects through industry events, networking, referrals, and research. Screening: Conduct an initial review of potential investments to assess their alignment with BlockShift Capital's investment.

02

Initial Evaluation

Assessment: Perform a preliminary evaluation of the startup’s business model, technology, market potential, team, and competitive landscape. Meetings: Hold introductory meetings with the founders to understand their vision, strategy

03

Due Diligence

Technical Due Diligence: Examine the technical aspects of the project, including blockchain architecture, security, scalability,. Market Analysis: Evaluate the market opportunity, target audience, competitive environment, and potential for growth.

04

Investment Committee Review

Presentation: Prepare a detailed investment memo and presentation for the investment committee, summarizing findings. Decision: The investment committee reviews the proposal and makes a decision based on strategic fit.

05

Term Sheet and Negotiation

Term Sheet: If approved, draft a term sheet outlining the terms and conditions of the investment, including valuation, investment amount, equity stake, and investor rights.

06

Post-Investment Support

Strategic Guidance: Provide ongoing strategic advice and mentorship to help the startup achieve its goals and navigate challenges. Networking: Leverage BlockShift Capital’s network to connect the startup with industry experts, partners, and potential customers.

BlockShift Capital is a venture capital firm that specializes in investing in blockchain technology and digital assets. The firm focuses on supporting early-stage startups and projects that leverage blockchain technology to create innovative solutions and drive the adoption of decentralized systems. BlockShift Capital provides funding, strategic guidance, and networking opportunities to help these companies grow and succeed in the rapidly evolving blockchain and cryptocurrency industry.

Blockchain Technology: Investing in companies that develop and implement blockchain solutions across various industries, including finance, supply chain, healthcare, and more.


Digital Assets: Supporting projects that create or utilize digital assets such as cryptocurrencies, tokens, and other blockchain-based financial instruments.

Decentralized Finance (DeFi): Backing platforms and protocols that offer decentralized financial services, such as lending, borrowing, trading, and yield farming, without traditional intermediaries.

NFTs and Digital Collectibles: Investing in projects related to non-fungible tokens (NFTs), which represent ownership of unique digital items such as art, music, and virtual real estate.

Web3 and Decentralized Applications (dApps): Supporting the development of Web3 technologies and decentralized applications that provide new ways for users to interact with digital services in a decentralized manner.

Infrastructure and Tools: Funding companies that build the infrastructure and tools necessary for the blockchain ecosystem to thrive, including scalability solutions, interoperability protocols, and developer tools.

RegTech and LegalTech: Investing in technologies that address regulatory and legal challenges within the blockchain and cryptocurrency space, ensuring compliance and enhancing security.

Gaming and Metaverse: Supporting blockchain-based gaming platforms and virtual worlds that utilize blockchain technology for in-game assets, economies, and player interactions.

"Shift": Implies a significant change or transformation. In this context, it suggests a shift in the financial and technological landscape brought about by blockchain technology. It also conveys the firm's mission to drive and support this transformative shift through their investments.

"Capital": Indicates that the firm is a venture capital entity, providing financial resources and strategic support to startups and projects within the blockchain ecosystem.
